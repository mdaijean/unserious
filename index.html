Hi, thanks for checking out my little website! My name's Ameenah, I'm a 23-year-old aspiring Software Developer based in Detroit, MI, and I'm really excited to learn how to code professionally!

How I Got My Start:

I think that, like most of my generation that developed a career affinity for software engineering, I came in contact with all of it (HTML, CSS, JavaScript) fairly early. Exposure to software engineering, IMO, was made highly accessible through social media platforms like Tumblr and MySpace, where a person could tweak the code of their blog (Tumblr) and profile (MySpace). Like I stated prior, because I grew up during the late stage of the social media boom, I think my coming into the Tech industry by way of software engineering was a preeminent thing.

I came across Grand Circus on Instagram via an alumni named Brea. Already familiar vaguely with coding through my teen years spending countless hours (in vain, usually, because I wasn't THAT familiar with software engineering as a whole!) trying to make my blog look pretty and whatnot, and innately curious, I signed up to take a Grand Circus Bootcamp Cohort and here I am! I'm extremely excited for this journey, too. At first it was anxiety-inducing, but I've gotten over that. It's time to utilize Grand Circus to assist in pursuing my dream career!
